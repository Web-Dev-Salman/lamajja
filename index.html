COURSES

We can bring not only experienced artists to an excellent and professional level, but also complete beginners.

In our portfolio you will find a basic accredited retraining course for permanent makeup, as well as original more demanding techniques for advanced artists.

A well-developed training system combined with an online platform and the trainer’s interest in the students even after the course ensures that you really learn the techniques.

You can find testimonials from our students about the courses here.

Why study with us?

  • The permanent makeup courses at LaMajja Academy are no ordinary courses. We are interested in students even after the course, with no time limit.

  • We teach not only permanent makeup at the highest level, but also how to build a successful business in this area. How to make a name for yourself and secure clients.

  • We give the best students the opportunity to present themselves under the LaMajja logo.

  • We have prepared programs for complete beginners as well as advanced artists, on various techniques.

  • Limited number of students on each course for an individual approach to each student.

HOW ARE THE COURSES AT THE LAMAJJA ACADEMY?

THEORY

In the introductory part you can expect ONLINE video lessons, which you will study from the comfort of your home so that you are perfectly prepared for the course theoretically and then we can devote more time to practice, which is the most important in this field.

LIVE COURSES

The beginners’ courses are suitable not only for students who are new to the word permanent makeup, but also for artists who have been working with permanent makeup for some time and feel that it’s simply “not right” and want to improve.
These courses last between 2 – 5 days depending on the type of course.

PMU BASIC (without VAT)

  • WEEKLY PREPARATION COURSE ONLINE - You study from the video materials and do the assignments given by Veronika.

  • 5 DAYS AT LAMAJJA ACADEMY - Theory, work on exercise latex, work on live models.

  • INDEPENDENT SUPPORT AFTER COURSE - We care about our students. That's why we are always open to communication and consultation for you after graduation. During the following 6 months after the course, you will receive unlimited individual support from LaMajja trainers.

  • INDIVIDUAL APPROACH - Limited number of students for individual approach to each student.

  • OPPORTUNITY TO GET LAMAJJA LOGO AND PLACEMENT ON LAMAJJA OPPORTUNITY MAP - The best students have the opportunity to be part of the LaMajja team.

  • STARTING PACKAGE INCLUDED IN THE COURSE PRICE - We care about the quality of the products we use for our students' future clients.

  • 1 YEAR ACCESS TO ONLINE MATERIALS - We know that some information is better heard or seen several times, so you can always come back to the information from our course and repeat the entire course.

A basic course in permanent make-up, on the basis of which you will obtain a professional exam and then you can open a business.

  • 10 days of intensive permanent make-up training. 5 days of online preparation from the comfort of your home and 5 days at the LaMajja Academy in Roudnice nad Labem.
  • You will learn 3 basic PMU techniques - shaded lips, shaded eyebrows, classic eyeliner.
  • Also suitable for advanced artists who want to improve in these techniques.
  • The course can be completed with an accredited professional exam, based on which you can open a permanent makeup business (Violation of the integrity of human skin / Permanent Makeup Expert).
  • We teach our students not only how to do permanent makeup, but also how to properly start their PMU career, how to have a full diary and be successful in marketing, which is an essential part of this business.
  • The course includes a starter pack with LaMajja original products
  • Certificate of completion of the LaMajja course

DAY 1 10:00 - 18:00

  • Introduction to PMU
  • Anatomy and physiology of the skin
  • Hygienic and legal requirements for the performance of PMU
  • Contraindications
  • Colour theory, Pigmentology, Fitzpatrick scale
  • Necessary materials for every PMU artist
  • Introduction to the permanent machine
  • Practical training of individual strokes on the training latex
  • Thorough description of the PMU lip technique and practice on practice latex

2. DAY 10:00 - 18:00

  • Thorough description of the PMU technique for shaded eyebrows and eyeliner and practice on the practice latex
    including the procedure of measuring the form
  • Types of machines and needles-advantages and disadvantages for shaded eyebrow and eyeliner technique

3. DAY 10:00 - 18:00

  • Preparing the client before the procedure
  • Color and pigment theory for permanent lip makeup
  • Working on a live model - lips

4. DAY 10:00 - 18:00

  • Client preparation before the procedure
  • Colour and pigment theory for shaded eyebrows
  • Live model work - shaded eyebrows

5. DAY 10:00 - 18:00

  • Preparing the client before the procedure
  • Colour and pigment theory for eyeliner
  • Working on a live model - classic eyeliner
  • Final exam
  • Presentation of certificates

As part of the course, each student will receive a free starter package worth 5000 CZK / 200 EUR.

  • Targeting thread - white
  • Targeting thread - black
  • PMU pencil - white
  • PMU pencil - black
  • Exercise latex
  • Disposable adhesive ruler for measuring eyebrow shape
  • LaMajja work apron
  • Applicators
  • Microbrushes
  • Pigment cups
